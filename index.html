about

Hello, there! I'm Kelly Talley, a freelance graphic designer and web developer based in Birmingham, Alabama. I specialize in design and digital products for individuals and small businesses. I have over a decade of design experience, and I am passionate about designing creative and effective products.

When I'm not creating fabulous digital and print designs, I'm often creating something delicious in my kitchen or something warm and fuzzy with my knitting needles. Or maybe I'm outside on the trail in my hiking boots or on my bike.

If you have a project you'd like me to work on, or if you just want to say hello, I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to fill out the contact form below or email me at kelly.c.talley@gmail.com.

TEDxBirmingham Save the Date Card

Designed for the 2016 TEDxBirmingham event. Going on the theme of "Pure Imagination," I created the idea lightbulb with the filaments in the shape of an X, a common design feature for TEDx events. Also, I used the X motif in the overall design of the card.

First Presbyterian Church

Logo and WordPress website and created for First Presbyterian Church in Eufaula, Alabama. This small-town church needed a website with an updated look. The logo design is inspired by one of the many stained-glass windows in the church's sanctuary. View the full site at firstpresbyterianeufaula.com.

Pomodoro Timer

Timer project built using AngularJS and Firebase. It uses the Pomodoro method, with a 25-minute timer and 5 minute breaks in between pomodoros. View the full app here.

Marathon Marketing

Marathon Electrical Contractors came to me with a need for a full update of their marketing materials.
